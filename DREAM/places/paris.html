Paris

The City of Love

Back to Dream Page

I’ve only seen Paris in movies and series, especially Emily in Paris. I want to experience that romantic, soft, aesthetic lifestyle — walking around in cute outfits, drinking coffee in a café, and seeing the city lights at night. I also dream of seeing “Emily’s apartment” vibe in real life and taking photos near iconic Paris streets.

What I want to see & experience:

  • Eiffel Tower at night
  • Parisian cafés and cobblestone streets
  • Classic Haussmann-style apartments
