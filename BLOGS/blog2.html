From Silent Viewer to the Girl on Your Screen

BY: JANA SAYRIELLE G. ABORDO | JANUARY 21, 2026

Introduction | Body | Conclusion

Dati, tinatawanan ko lang ang mga influencer na napapanood ko online. I was just another viewer, endlessly scrolling through TikTok, watching makeup routines, daily vlogs, and Shopee hauls lalo na kapag may piso sale. Pero habang pinapanood ko sila, may tanong palaging bumabalik sa isip ko: Paano nila nagagawa magsalita sa harap ng camera nang walang takot? Saan nanggagaling yung confidence nila? I never imagined that one day, I would become someone brave enough to press “record” myself.

Where It All Began

Stepping Outside My Comfort Zone

The truth is, ayaw ko talagang humarap sa camera noon. Mababa ang confidence ko and I was terrified of judgment. Sensitive akong tao, and takot na takot akong mapintasan or ma-bash. Pero my dad saw something in me that I couldn’t see in myself yet. Lagi niyang sinasabi, kung maarte raw ako sa sarili ko, sa damit, sa mukha, then why not try creating content like the people I admired?

Napilitan akong mag-download ng TikTok, but I hated seeing myself on screen. Every photo and video felt awkward and uncomfortable. Paulit-ulit kong iniisip kung ano ang sasabihin ng ibang tao. Pero one day, I posted a simple lip-sync video. Walang effort, walang expectation. Bigla itong nagkaroon ng libong likes. Napatanong na lang ako sa sarili ko, “Ganito lang pala?”

Compliments started coming in. Dumami ang followers ko, even from my other social media accounts. Inaamin ko, nagustuhan ko yung attention. Sino bang hindi? Akala ko pagtatawanan lang ako, pero mali pala ako. Minsan, yung mga takot lang natin ang pinakamalakas humila pababa sa atin.

When Things Fell Apart

Balancing Reality and Dreams

Habang tumatagal, nagsimula nang mag-reach out ang brands. Hindi ako makapaniwala na posible palang kumita ng pera sa pagpo-post ng content. Everything felt exciting—until face-to-face classes started. Doon ko naramdaman kung gaano kahirap i-balance ang pag-aaral at content creation. Unti-unti akong nawalan ng consistency. Huminto ang videos, bumaba ang likes, hanggang sa tuluyan ko nang iniwan ang account ko.

Mas masakit pa, sumabay pa ang online bashing. Doon talaga naubos ang gana ko. Hanggang ngayon, may parte sa akin na nanghihinayang. Pero siguro, kailangan ko munang huminto para mas makilala ang sarili ko.

Starting Again, This Time for Myself

Rediscovering Purpose

Recently, I decided to try again. This time, mas totoo na ako sa sarili ko. I explored different styles—lip-syncs, dancing, fashion, skincare, and beauty content—without pressuring myself to be perfect. Surprisingly, things started to fall into place. Hindi lang confidence ang bumalik, pati rin ang sense of purpose.

What makes this journey even more meaningful is that content creation helped me support myself financially. Since senior high school hanggang ngayon, I’ve been helping pay my own bills. Hindi siya madali, pero proud ako sa sarili ko. Lahat ng ito, bunga ng sipag at lakas ng loob.

Lessons I Carry With Me

January 2026

This journey taught me that people will always have something to say. May tatawa, may manghuhusga, at may maniniwala sa’yo. Pero sa huli, ang mahalaga ay kung paano ka tatayo para sa sarili mo. I learned that growth often starts from discomfort, and confidence is built by showing up—even when you’re scared.

If you’re thinking of entering this path, do it scared. Do it unsure. Just don’t let fear stop you from trying. Because sometimes, all it takes is one brave step to change how you see yourself.
